About

Get To Know Me

My Journey With English

Teaching English has been my passion since 2019, and it's been an incredibly fulfilling experience. My academic background includes a Bachelor's degree in English Language and Literature, which laid the foundation for my love of language and literature. Building on that, I pursued a Master's degree in English and American Studies, delving deeper into the intricacies of language, culture, and literature.


Throughout my journey, I've developed a profound appreciation for languages and their ability to connect people from diverse backgrounds. I find joy in helping others unlock the power of language, whether it's through teaching grammar rules, exploring literature, or practicing conversational skills.


Each day as an English educator is a new opportunity to inspire and empower my students to achieve their language learning goals. I believe in creating a supportive and dynamic learning environment where students feel motivated to explore, experiment, and grow in their language proficiency.


As I continue on this journey, I look forward to sharing my knowledge, passion, and enthusiasm for English with learners from around the world, helping them embark on their own paths to fluency and linguistic discovery.
